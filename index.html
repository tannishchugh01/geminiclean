How do I remove the Gemini logo from a video?
Upload your Gemini-generated video to GeminiClean, click "Remove Gemini Logo", and download the clean version. The entire process happens in your browser using ffmpeg — no data is sent anywhere.

About

GeminiClean is a free browser-based tool to remove the Gemini AI sparkle logo (✦) from videos generated using Google Gemini and Veo. No server, no signup, no cost.

How It Works

We use ffmpeg.wasm — a WebAssembly port of FFmpeg — running entirely in your browser. Your video is never sent anywhere. Your privacy is guaranteed by design.
